Privacy Policy – VIN Explorer

Effective Date: 7/24/2025

VIN Explorer does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Handling

This app allows users to manually enter or scan a Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) to retrieve vehicle details. The VIN is sent to a third-party decoding service for processing, but no personal data is attached to or collected during this process.

Third-Party Services

The app may use external APIs to decode VINs. These APIs may receive the VIN entered by the user but do not receive any identifying information about the user or their device.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users 18 years of age and older. It is not designed for children and does not target or knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. All updates will be posted to this page. Continued use of the app after changes are made implies acceptance of the updated policy.
